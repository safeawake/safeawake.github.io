Drowsiness Detection & Alerting System

In our graduation project, we focused on "drowsy driving", which is one of the causes of traffic accidents in daily life. SafeAway aims to accurately detect signs of drowsiness by monitoring the driver's facial expressions and eyelid movement. When the system detects signs of drowsiness, it warns the driver with audible alarms, reducing the possibility of accidents that may occur due to drowsy driving. In this way, our project will contribute to drivers having a safer driving experience and preventing traffic accidents.
